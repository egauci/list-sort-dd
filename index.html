Keyboard Instructions

  • Use tab to move to and between lists
  • Arrow down to go to the first item in the list
  • Arrow down or arrow up to move within the list
  • Space to grab an item
  • Arrow down/up to move to a drop target
  • Space, enter, or ^M to drop the grabbed item
  • Esc to abort a move
